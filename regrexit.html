Regrexit

Specification of the piece:

Laser cut Sticky vinyl on Santander Bike.


Description:

This project was released two weeks before the 29th of March, before Brexit was due to leave. The stickers are designed to be on the Santander Cycle. Reasons. 1.Is a public bicycle hire scheme in London. 2.A cycle is a symbol of free-movement. 3.The scheme is sponsored by Santander (Spanish Bank) from April 2015. 4.The cycle is known as Boris Bikes since was launched by him. 5.Communication tool.
