Latinos are very serviceable and people here like that

Specification of the piece:

Water-based repellent on concrete.


Description:

This project is research-led from a series of interviews to all of the Royal College of Art cleaners. When I started at RCA, I realised the outsourced cleaners were from different South American's countries (Colombia, Ecuador and Bolivia). Since then, I have been looking at the interrelation between emigrants coming from these countries and the service industry within London. Another portrait of post-colonialism.
